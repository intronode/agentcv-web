# PRODUCT-BAR.md — the quality contract

# Referenced by the goal conditions. Commit at docs/PRODUCT-BAR.md.

# Shape-agnostic on purpose: it constrains HOW GOOD, not WHAT.

## The test

Put the product beside Linear and Vercel (finish/polish) and beside
AngelList and culinaryagents.com (information density, completeness of a
marketplace/HR surface). A stranger landing on it must believe **a funded
team launched this** — not that a prototype is being demoed. Early
completion against a thin product is a FAILURE state, not efficiency.

## Rubric — self-score each dimension 1–5 every cycle; launch bar = no

## dimension below 4

1. **First-impression credibility**: landing communicates the thesis in
   10 seconds; visual identity is distinctive, not template-default.
2. **Information density with hierarchy**: screens reward scanning AND
   reading; no decorative emptiness; data tables/cards carry real fields.
3. **Depth behind every click**: each primary surface leads somewhere
   substantive; zero dead ends, zero "coming soon" on CORE paths.
4. **Interaction completeness**: search, filter, sort, compare (or the
   shape's equivalents) actually work against real data; forms validate;
   errors are designed.
5. **Honesty as craft**: provenance labels, [unknown] fields, and
   trust-tier logic rendered as polished product language, not
   disclaimers.
6. **Performance & soundness**: prod-mode navigation smooth; no console
   errors; responsive at laptop + mobile widths.
7. **Copy quality**: every headline/empty-state/CTA reads like a product
   writer wrote it; zero lorem-ipsum-grade filler.

## Floors (measurable minimums, any shape)

- **No empty rooms**: every list/directory surface renders ≥8 substantive
  entries at launch; detail pages have all sections populated.
- **Seed density**: ≥30 substantive entities total across types, built in
  three honest layers — (a) REAL: Ari Collective + our verified data;
  (b) CURATED: configurations/patterns documented from cited public
  sources (link the source on the entity); (c) ILLUSTRATIVE: clearly
  labeled. No fourth layer. Nothing invented-as-real.
- **The flagship**: Ari Collective page is the single best page on the
  site — topology, windowed metrics with provenance, lessons, cost
  honesty.
- **Comparison is first-class**: whatever the shape, a user can put ≥2
  configurations/subjects side by side and learn something.
- **The thesis is on the surface**: a visitor can learn what harness
  engineering is and why composition matters without leaving the site
  (an explainer surface — /trust evolved, or equivalent).
- **Conversion path**: a visitor can take the wedge action (request
  setup / submit their configuration / claim) end-to-end with persistence.

## Critique-cycle protocol (mandatory, ≥3 full cycles before DONE)

Each cycle, surfaced in the transcript: (1) screenshot every CORE surface
at desktop + mobile widths; (2) score the rubric 1–7 with one-line
justifications, harshest-plausible-critic voice; (3) name the single
weakest thing on the site; (4) fix it and what surrounds it; (5) re-shoot.
Cycles with no score below the previous cycle's minimum don't count
toward the three. The evaluator only sees what you surface — surface the
scores, the screenshots' filenames, and the deltas.
